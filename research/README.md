## Research Directions

Building on the foundational paper, our active research areas include:
- **Mycelium-inspired network topologies** — Decentralized routing and resilience patterns derived from fungal architectures
- **Bio-hybrid sensor integration** — TinyML nodes interfacing with living fungal substrates
- **Privacy-preserving federated learning** — Distributed model training across edge nodes without centralized data collection
- **Ecological monitoring** — Long-term data collection on soil moisture, biodiversity, and ecosystem health in high-desert environments
