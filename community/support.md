# Support Luminis Foundation

Luminis Foundation is a nonprofit research organization building sovereign, ecologically-situated AI for the public good.

## Why Support Matters

Your support advances:
- Open-access scientific research at the intersection of AI, ecology, and decentralized systems
- Field deployment of bio-hybrid sensor networks in the Pecos watershed
- Public education materials on ethical, privacy-preserving technology
- Open-source tools and protocols for community-led deployments

## How to Support

### Contribute Your Skills
The most immediate way to support the foundation is through direct contribution — research, documentation, code, or community building. See [Get Involved](get-involved.md).

### Share the Work
Help spread awareness of the foundation's research and mission:
- Share the [foundational paper](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.20143391) with colleagues
- Star and share this repository
- Connect us with aligned researchers, institutions, and funders

### Financial Support
Formal donation channels are being established as part of the 501(c)(3) application process. Details will be published here once available.
